# HTTP Status CODE:
--- 100: Informational

--- 200: Success
--- 201: Created - New resource successfully created (e.g. after a POST request)

--- 300: Redirection
--- 301: Moved Permanently

Client ERROR
--- 400: Bad request - The request is malformed or invalid.
--- 401: Unauthorized - You must log in (missing or invalid credintials).
--- 403: Forbidden - You're not allow to access this site.
--- 404: Not Found - The URL doesn't exist.
--- 429: Too Many Requests

Server ERROR
--- 500: Internal Server ERROR - Something borke on the server.
--- 503: Service Unavailable - Server is temporarily overloaded or down.
